# About Us

Located in the most popular area of Keszthely, just a stone's throw from Lake Balaton, we welcome our guests at 2 charming guesthouses, situated only 100 meters apart – open throughout the whole year.

As a family-run business, we have shaped our unique identity through many years of experience in the world of tourism. With a keen eye for detail and a focus on what truly matters, we create a refined, welcoming atmosphere where our guests can feel completely at ease.

Spotless cleanliness, attentiveness, and careful hospitality are our core values – offering a peaceful, stylish, and friendly environment for a refreshing stay by the lake.

Each guesthouse features 11 rooms, mostly double rooms: Villa Lillybeth accommodates up to 25 guests, while Lillybeth Lakeside comfortably hosts 22, making them ideal for both smaller and larger groups.

Thanks to our prime location, both guesthouses are easily accessible by public transport or car. The nearby bus and train stations make your arrival simple and convenient. Free parking is available on site. EV charging possibilities are provided at both locations, additional fees apply. Cyclists can safely store their bikes. Bike rentals are also available nearby, making it easy to actively explore the area.

Our rooms are air-conditioned, most with balconies or terraces, and feature high-quality, comfortable mattresses to ensure a relaxing stay. Fresh linens, soft towels, toiletries, free Wi-Fi, and cable TV are provided. Well-considered details throughout the rooms make our guests feel at home and ensure a peaceful, restorative experience.

**Lillybeth is more than just an accommodation – it's a place to slow down, recharge, and truly experience the tranquility of Lake Balaton.**

## Villa Lillybeth – Elegant Comfort Near the Lake

Villa Lillybeth is a renovated, historic villa where classic charm meets modern comfort. It is located just 300 meters from Lake Balaton and a short walk from the historic town centre.

The guesthouse features 11 individually accessible rooms (9 double, 1 triple, 1 quadruple), making it perfect for couples or smaller groups seeking relaxation and quality leisure.

The villa's charming garden includes an outdoor kitchen, and a fully equipped communal indoor kitchen is also available – perfect for longer stays.

*Romantic atmosphere, historic charm, and modern comfort – that's Villa Lillybeth.*

## Lillybeth Lakeside – Fresh, Modern Comfort by the Lake

Lillybeth Lakeside is our guesthouse closest to Lake Balaton, freshly renovated with modern design and thoughtfully planned interiors for a peaceful, high-quality stay.

Bright, harmonious spaces and practical solutions put comfort first, making it an ideal choice for groups of friends and smaller parties.

The proximity to the lake is noticeable at every moment – just a short walk, and you're at the shoreside.

*Freshness, simplicity, and the authentic lakeside experience – this is Lillybeth Lakeside.*

## Keszthely Attractions

### Historical & Cultural

- Festetics Palace – iconic Baroque palace with Helikon Library and stunning gardens
- Balaton Museum – regional history, folklore, and natural heritage
- Georgikon Agricultural Museum – Hungary's first agricultural school
- Castle Park – romantic stroll near Festetics Palace

### Lakefront Experiences

- Keszthely City Beach – family-friendly, water activities
- Balaton Pier & Marina – sunsets, boat trips, walking
- Lakeside Promenade – evening strolls, cafes, beautiful views

## Nearby Excursions

### Half-day trips

- Hévíz Thermal Lake – spa waters, lakeside walk, refreshing experiences
- Fenyves Nature Trail – easy hike, birdwatching, plant spotting

### Full-day trips

- Keszthely Hills – viewpoints, forest trails
- Kis-Balaton Nature Trail – bird reserve, observation points
- Szigliget Castle – panoramic lake views
- Sümeg Castle – medieval fortress, knight tournaments
- Badacsony Wine Region – wine tastings and cellar tours
- Folly Arboretum – diverse conifer and ornamental plant collection
- Boating and sailing around Keszthely

### Active Fun

- Balaton Bob (Gyenesdiás) – short, adventurous fun
- Adventure parks – active programs for all ages
- Cycling along the lake – half- or full-day trips
